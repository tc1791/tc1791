We run rivers to relax

We believe that there is nothing like a river trip for relaxing. Sometimes a slow river rafting trip is just the thing to really take in the beautiful world God created for us.

We run whitewater for the rush

That doesn't mean we don't also like rapids. Moderation in all good things after all. The challenging times in life, and in rafting, make it easier to appreciate the easy times.

Come run with us!

Whether you want a challenging or relaxing rafting trip, we invite you to come with us on one of the amazing rivers we visit. Check out our trips and let us know which one best suits you. We're waiting to hear from you!
